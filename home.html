#SDE2017

Synaesthesia Dance Experience premiered October 1st 2017 at the Actors Gang Theatre in Culver City, CA as one of many exciting events during a two month synaesthesia international exhibition entitled:
Synaesthesia: What is the taste of the color blue.

SDE is the first of its kind to showcase synesthete dancers, visual artists, choreographers, and multi media art as an immersive 4D multisensory show for the audience. What is synesthesia?
